Content Attribution & Disclaimers

1. Original Content Statement

All lesson guides, study materials, quizzes, and instructional content on PeerLearn were created by the project team for educational and demonstration purposes. This includes:

  • Lesson Content: Algebra Guide, Geometry Guide, Biology Guide, Chemistry Guide, Essay Writing Guide, Grammar Guide, History Guide, and US History Guide
  • Quiz Questions & Answers: All quiz content across Mathematics, Science, English, and History subjects
  • Platform Features: Dashboard, progress tracking system, achievement badges, and interactive learning interface
  • Custom Content Creation: Admin panel tools allowing educators to create custom lessons, quizzes, videos, and materials

These materials are original works created by Karthikeya Konduri and Andrey Bond for the FBLA 2026 competition.

2. Curriculum Alignment & Standards

PeerLearn content topics align with commonly taught high school curriculum areas:

  • Mathematics: Algebra Essentials, Geometry Fundamentals
  • Science: Biology Basics, Chemistry Fundamentals
  • English Language Arts: Grammar Basics, Essay Writing
  • History & Social Studies: World History, US History

3. External Media Credits

Educational videos are sourced from publicly available YouTube content and remain the property of their respective creators. All embedded videos are used with proper attribution.

4. Usage Disclaimer

All content on PeerLearn is used solely for educational demonstration purposes within the FBLA 2026 competition context.
