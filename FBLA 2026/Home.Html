FAQs

Answers to questions you might have about PeerLearn.

What is PeerLearn?

PeerLearn is a student-led learning hub offering peer tutoring, study groups, and interactive resources.

Who is PeerLearn for?

Students of all levels who want to learn together.

How secure is my data?

Security is a priority — we recommend using secure meeting links for real-time sessions.

Is it free?

Yes — PeerLearn is free and student-run.
